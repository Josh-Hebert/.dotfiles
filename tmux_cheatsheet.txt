split window vertically Ctrl+b "
split window horizontally Ctrl+b %
close current window Ctrl+b &
Close current pane Ctrl+b x
Open new Window/tab Ctrl+b c
Rename current window Ctrl+b 
Switch windows Ctrl+b p (previous) or Ctrl+b n (next),
Connect to specific window Ctrl+b t "session name or number" or tmux -a t session name or number
list sessions tmux list-sessions or tmux ls or Ctrl+b s
Rename a session tmux rename-session "Session Name" or just a $
